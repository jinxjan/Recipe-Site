Goblin Gorp

Description

This recipe is my favvvoorrate. I have to stop myself from gobbling up the sweets while mixing. Yummyy, quick, and has all my favorite nicknack snacks.

Ingredients

  • 2.5 quarts popped popcorn
  • 2 cups small pretzels
  • 2 cups raisins
  • 1 cup salted peanuts
  • 1 cup M&M's
  • 1 cup candy corn

Directions

  1. In large bowl, combine all ingredients.
  2. Toss aggressively.
